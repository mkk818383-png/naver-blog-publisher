# V2 draft modes and disclosure contract

## Modes

- `model-draft`: a revisable working draft. Any model-created personal-sounding
  detail is marked `MODEL-DRAFT` and must not be presented as verified user
  experience.
- `publish-safe`: an export candidate. First-person experience is limited to
  user-provided evidence and all research gates must be complete.
- `memo-only`: facts, links, and uncertainties for internal review only; it is
  never compiled or exported as a post.

## Disclosure matrix

| mode | disclosure_kind | disclosure_text | disclosure_placement | export rule |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| model-draft | draft marker | MODEL-DRAFT | file and report header | human revision first |
| publish-safe, self-paid | self-paid | truthful self-paid statement when applicable | final disclosure area | human review required |
| publish-safe, sponsored | sponsored | material-connection statement | title or opening and final disclosure area | human review required |
| memo-only | internal memo | not public | memo header | export prohibited |

Sponsored disclosure is required in the title or opening and again in the final
disclosure area. Ordinary model assistance has no universal public label; the
matrix applies only to applicable sponsorship or similar required disclosure.

## Manual publication boundary

Validation may produce an export package, but it cannot publish to Naver or
any other service. A human performs the final review, chooses publication, and
manually publishes the approved export.
